## Travel

<div class="bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-md p-6 mb-8">
  <div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-6">
    <div>
      <h3 class="font-medium text-gray-500 uppercase text-sm tracking-wider mb-1">By Air</h3>
      <p class="text-gray-800 mb-2">The best way to travel is <a href="https://www.flightconnections.com/flights-to-lanzarote-arrecife-ace" class="text-primary">via a plane to Lanzarote airport</a>.</p>
      <p class="text-gray-600 text-sm">Flights from other Canary Islands (Tenerife/Gran Canaria) are cheap. Flights from Madrid and Barcelona are even cheaper, around 20 EUR.</p>
    </div>
    
    <div>
      <h3 class="font-medium text-gray-500 uppercase text-sm tracking-wider mb-1">Local Transport</h3>
      <p class="text-gray-800 mb-2">From the Lanzarote airport you can take a taxi to the venue, they have a flat rate around 20-25 EUR.</p>
      <p class="text-gray-600 text-sm"><a href="https://autoreisen.com/" class="text-primary">Renting a car</a> is usually quite cheap, around 200-350 EUR for the week.</p>
